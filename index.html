Tips

  • Fish don't like their glass being tapped
  • Watch out for algae growth in the tanks
  • Find fish that will get along together
  • Don't let them die
  • Tell them that you love them
  • Keep fish in water
  • Most fish are carnivores so be careful when mixing different types of fish
  • Make sure the fish have plenty of space to swim in
  • Do not go swimming with your fish
